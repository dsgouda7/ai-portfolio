Q: What are your delivery hours?
A: We deliver from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM, 7 days a week. Extended hours on Friday and Saturday until midnight.

Q: How long does delivery take?
A: Average delivery time is 30-45 minutes depending on your location and current order volume. We'll provide an estimated time when you place your order.

Q: Do you offer gluten-free options?
A: Yes! We offer gluten-free crusts for all our pizzas at no extra charge. Please mention this when ordering.

Q: Can I customize my pizza?
A: Absolutely! You can add extra toppings, remove ingredients, or request special preparations. Additional toppings are $1.50 each.

Q: What sizes do you offer?
A: We offer three sizes: Small (10"), Medium (12"), and Large (14"). Prices vary by size.

Q: Do you have vegan options?
A: Yes! We offer vegan cheese and have several vegetable-based pizzas. Just ask for vegan cheese when ordering.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and cash on delivery. Online payment is also available.

Q: Is there a minimum order for delivery?
A: Yes, our minimum order for delivery is $15 before tax and delivery fee.

Q: Can I track my order?
A: Yes! Once your order is placed, you'll receive a tracking link via SMS or email where you can see real-time updates.

Q: Do you offer contactless delivery?
A: Yes, we offer contactless delivery. Just mention this preference when placing your order.

Q: What if I have food allergies?
A: Please inform us of any allergies when ordering. We'll take extra care with your order. However, our kitchen handles common allergens.

Q: Can I schedule a delivery for later?
A: Yes! You can schedule deliveries up to 24 hours in advance when ordering online or by phone.

Q: Do you cater for events?
A: Yes! We offer catering services for events. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance for large orders (10+ pizzas).

Q: What's your refund policy?
A: If you're not satisfied with your order, please call us within 30 minutes of delivery. We'll offer a full refund or replacement.

Q: Are your pizzas halal?
A: Our chicken and beef options are halal-certified. Please ask when ordering to ensure proper handling.
