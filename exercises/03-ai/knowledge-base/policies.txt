DELIVERY POLICY

Delivery Area:
We deliver within a 5-mile radius of our location. Delivery fee is $2.99 for orders under $25. Free delivery on orders over $25.

Delivery Times:
- Monday to Thursday: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
- Friday to Saturday: 11:00 AM - 12:00 AM (Midnight)
- Sunday: 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM

Estimated delivery time is 30-45 minutes from order placement. During peak hours (6 PM - 8 PM), delivery may take up to 60 minutes.

Delivery Fee Structure:
- Under $25: $2.99 delivery fee
- $25 - $50: Free delivery
- Over $50: Free delivery + priority handling

Weather Delays:
During severe weather conditions, delivery times may be extended. We'll notify you if your order will be delayed by more than 15 minutes.


REFUND AND REPLACEMENT POLICY

Quality Guarantee:
If you're not satisfied with your order for any reason, please contact us within 30 minutes of delivery. We'll offer:
- Full refund, OR
- Complete replacement order

Valid Reasons for Refund/Replacement:
- Pizza arrived cold or undercooked
- Wrong order delivered
- Missing items from your order
- Quality issues (e.g., burnt pizza)
- Excessive delivery delay (over 60 minutes)

How to Request:
Call us at (555) 123-4567 or use the chat feature in your order confirmation email.


CANCELLATION POLICY

Cancellation Window:
Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placement for a full refund. After 5 minutes, the order is in preparation and cannot be cancelled.

How to Cancel:
- Online orders: Use the "Cancel Order" button in your confirmation email
- Phone orders: Call us immediately at (555) 123-4567

Refund Processing:
- Credit/debit card: 3-5 business days
- Cash orders: Immediate cancellation, no charge

Late Cancellation:
If you cancel after the 5-minute window or reject delivery without valid reason, a $5 preparation fee will be charged.


FOOD SAFETY AND ALLERGEN POLICY

Allergen Information:
Our kitchen handles the following allergens:
- Wheat (gluten)
- Dairy products
- Eggs
- Soy
- Tree nuts (in some desserts)

Cross-Contamination:
While we take precautions, we cannot guarantee complete allergen-free preparation due to shared kitchen equipment.

Special Dietary Requirements:
Please inform us of any allergies or dietary restrictions when ordering. We'll take extra care, but if you have severe allergies, please consider the cross-contamination risk.


PRIVACY POLICY

Data Collection:
We collect your name, phone number, delivery address, and payment information solely for order processing and delivery.

Data Usage:
Your information is used only for:
- Processing and delivering your order
- Sending order confirmations and updates
- Improving our service (anonymized data)

We never sell or share your personal information with third parties except payment processors.

Data Retention:
Order history is kept for 1 year for customer service purposes. You can request deletion by contacting us.
